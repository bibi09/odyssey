Components list
---------------

C1 : electrolytic capacitor 10 µF
C2 : tantalum capacitor 10 µF
C3 : electrolytic capacitor 470 µF
C4 : ceramic capacitor 15 pF
C5 : ceramic capacitor 15 pF

D1 : LED
D2 : LED

R1 : resistor 220 ohm 5%
R2 : resistor 10K ohm 5%
R3 : resistor 330 ohm 5%
R4 : resistor 1.8K ohm 5%
R5 : resistor 3.3K ohm 5%
R6 : resistor 1K ohm 5%
R7 : resistor 1K ohm 5%
R8 : resistor 1K ohm 5%

M1 : full rotation servomotor powered at 5V
M2 : full rotation servomotor powered at 5V
The servomotors used in the robot are DGServo S04NF STD.

U1 : linear voltage regulator LM1085-5.0 with output fixed at 5V
U2 : microcontroller PIC16F876

X1 : crystal 3.6864 MHz
